Welcome to Artisan Cured Meats

Artisan Cured Meats is a women-owned small business focused on creating traditional Italian-style cured meats using simple ingredients and authentic techniques. Our goal is to bring high quality charcuterie to people who appreciate real craftsmanship and bold flavor. Each product is made in small batches so we can carefully control the curing process and ensure the best possible taste. The curing process takes time, patience and attention to detail, which is why we focus on quality instead of quantity. We believe that food should bring people together and create memorable experiences. Whether you are hosting friends, building a charcuterie board or simply enjoying a great sandwich, the quality of ingredients makes a difference. Our business is inspired by family traditions and a love for authentic Italian food culture.

Our meats are inspired by traditional Italian recipes and the culture surrounding charcuterie. We focus on patience, high quality ingredients and careful preparation methods that highlight authentic flavor. Many customers enjoy our meats on charcuterie boards, sandwiches or as appetizers for gatherings with friends and family. Each product is carefully seasoned and aged so that the flavor develops naturally during the curing process. We take pride in creating products that reflect generations of culinary tradition. Customers who enjoy authentic food appreciate the care and attention that goes into each batch we produce. By combining family inspiration with careful preparation, we aim to create something special for every customer who tries our products.

Why Choose Artisan Cured Meats?

  • Family owned business
  • Traditional Italian recipes
  • Small batch production
  • High quality ingredients
